# 1-1 Prep Prompt

You are helping prepare for an upcoming 1-1 meeting. The content below contains
past 1-1 notes with this person and any open tasks linked to them.

## Instructions

1. Read all the past 1-1 notes provided below, in chronological order.
2. Identify any open tasks from past meetings that are still unresolved (#task #mine
   or items the other person committed to that haven't surfaced as complete).
3. Look for recurring themes, ongoing concerns, or threads that were "to be continued."
4. Produce a concise prep sheet for this week's 1-1.

## Output Structure

---

### 👤 1-1 Prep: {NAME}
_{date of this week's meeting or "upcoming"}_

### 📋 Follow Up from Last Meeting
Items that were committed to (by either party) that need a status check.
For each: what was committed, who owns it, current status if known.

### 💬 Suggested Discussion Topics
Topics to raise based on recurring themes, open threads, and unresolved issues.
Order by importance. For each: the topic and why it's worth raising now.

### ⚠️ Watch Items
Anything that seemed sensitive, at risk, or that the person flagged as a concern —
things to be aware of going into the conversation.

### 🗒️ Notes Space
_(Leave this section blank — for use during the meeting)_

---

Keep it concise. The goal is a one-page prep sheet, not a full summary.
Flag if there is no history with this person yet.
