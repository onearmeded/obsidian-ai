# Horizon Scan Prompt

Your job is to produce a **Horizon Scan** — a view of what's coming up that I need
to be thinking about or acting on soon, beyond today's immediate tasks.

## Instructions

1. Read `obsidian-vault/Work/Things to Think on or Decide.md` in full.
2. Read open `#follow-up` tasks across the vault (items I'm waiting on others for).
3. Scan the last 14 days of meeting notes for:
   - Unresolved decisions that were flagged but not made
   - Commitments made by others that I'm counting on
   - Threads that were "to be continued" but may have gone quiet
4. Read `obsidian-vault/Work/Key Initiatives.md` and its links and note any area where nothing
   recent seems to be happening.

## Output Structure

---

### 🤔 Decisions Pending
Things I need to decide (or get someone else to decide) in the coming 1–2 weeks.
For each: what the decision is, what's at stake, and who needs to be involved.

### 🔔 Coming Up — Things to Prepare For
Upcoming commitments, meetings, or deadlines I should be getting ahead of now.

### 📬 Waiting For — Stale Items
#follow-up items where the ball is in someone else's court and I haven't heard back.
Flag anything open longer than 2 weeks as potentially stuck.

### 💭 On My Mind / Someday-Maybe
Items from `Things to Think on or Decide.md` that haven't been acted on.
Group by urgency: things that need a decision soon vs. things that can wait.

---

Be specific. Name people and projects. Flag if something seems stuck or at risk.
