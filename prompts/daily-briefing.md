# Daily Briefing Prompt

The following content has been gathered from the vault. Your job is to produce a
**Daily Briefing** — a clear, prioritized view of what to focus on today.

## Instructions

1. Read the gathered tasks and recent meeting notes provided below.
2. Also read `obsidian-vault/Work/Things to Think on or Decide.md` for any hot items.
3. Produce output in the following structure:

---

### 🎯 Top Priorities Right Now
List the 3–5 most important next actions I should take today. For each, note:
- The specific action
- Why it's urgent or important (deadline, blocking someone, high-stakes)
- Which project it belongs to

### ✅ Full Next Actions List
All open #mine tasks, grouped by project. Flag any that are overdue (past due date).

### 👀 Waiting For — Needs a Nudge?
#follow-up items that have been open a long time or where something may be stuck.
Suggest which ones I should actively follow up on today.

### ⚡ From Recent Meetings
Any commitments, action items, or threads from the last 7 days that haven't
surfaced in the task list yet and deserve attention.

---

## Style
- Be direct and concrete. No padding.
- If something is truly urgent, say so clearly.
- If the list is short, that's fine — don't invent urgency.
